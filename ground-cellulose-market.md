<p><p>The ground cellulose market comprises two primary types: wood pulp-based and refined cotton-based. Wood pulp-based cellulose is derived from trees and is widely used in industries for its strength and fiber properties. It serves applications like papermaking and food additives. Refined cotton-based cellulose, on the other hand, originates from cotton fibers and is valued for its purity and absorbency, making it ideal for pharmaceuticals, cosmetics, and textiles. Both types play essential roles in various sectors, reflecting diverse applications and demand.</p></p>
