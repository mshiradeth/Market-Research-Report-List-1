<p><p>The Two-Wheeler Electric Fuel Injection Systems market primarily consists of two types: Electronic Fuel Injection (EFI) Systems and Carburetted Fuel Injection Systems. EFI systems utilize electronic controls to optimize fuel delivery, enhancing engine performance, efficiency, and emissions control. In contrast, Carburetted Fuel Injection Systems rely on mechanical components to mix air and fuel, offering a simpler setup but with reduced efficiency compared to EFI. The choice between these systems influences manufacturers' design strategies, performance metrics, and regulatory compliance.</p></p>
