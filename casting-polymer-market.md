<p><p>The **Casting Polymer Market** includes **Solid Surface**, **Engineered Stone**, and **Cultured Marble**, each serving distinct applications. **Solid Surface** is a non-porous, seamless material made from acrylic or polyester, ideal for countertops and sinks. **Engineered Stone** combines crushed quartz with resins, offering durability and a natural stone appearance, commonly used in high-end surfaces. **Cultured Marble** blends marble dust with resins, providing a cost-effective, customizable alternative for vanity tops and decorative items. All three cater to construction and interior design needs.</p></p>
