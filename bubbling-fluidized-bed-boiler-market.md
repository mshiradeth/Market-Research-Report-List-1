<p><p>A Bubbling Fluidized Bed Boiler is a type of industrial boiler that uses a fluidized bed of solid particles, typically sand or ash, to achieve efficient combustion of solid fuels. In this system, air is blown through the particle bed, causing it to behave like a fluid, enabling improved heat transfer and uniform temperature distribution.
