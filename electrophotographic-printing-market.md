<p><strong>The Electrophotographic Printing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Offset</li><li>Gravure</li><li>Flexography</li><li>Screen</li><li>Letterpress</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electrophotographic printing market encompasses various traditional printing techniques, each serving distinct applications. Offset printing utilizes plates to transfer ink onto substrates, ideal for high-volume production. Gravure employs engraved cylinders for rich, detailed images, commonly used in packaging. Flexography, with its flexible relief plates, is efficient for continuous patterns, often found in labels and packaging. Screen printing uses mesh to transfer ink onto surfaces, suitable for textiles and promotional materials. Letterpress relies on raised surfaces to create prints, offering a tactile quality, ideal for specialty items.</p></p>
