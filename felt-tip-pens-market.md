<p><p>The felt tip pen market can be categorized into three main types: oily, water-based, and alcoholic felt-tip pens. Oily felt-tip pens use oil-based ink that offers vibrant colors and is resistant to water and fading, making them ideal for outdoor use. Water-based felt-tip pens are made with a more environmentally friendly ink, providing easier cleanup and suitable for art and school projects. Alcoholic felt-tip pens contain alcohol-based ink, which dries quickly and delivers smooth, professional-quality lines, often used in graphic design and illustrations.</p></p>
