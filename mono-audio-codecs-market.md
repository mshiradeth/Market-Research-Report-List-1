<p><p>The mono audio codecs market can be divided into two main types: analog and digital. Analog codecs process audio signals in their natural continuous form, commonly used in traditional sound systems and older technology. In contrast, digital codecs convert audio signals into binary data, enabling efficient storage and transmission in modern devices like smartphones and computers. The digital market has gained prominence due to its superior compression capabilities, higher fidelity, and compatibility with modern streaming and communication technologies.</p></p>
