<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Ethylene Vinyl Acetate Resins Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vinyl Acetate-modified Polyethylene (Low VA Density)</li><li>Thermoplastic Ethylene Vinyl Acetate (Medium VA Density)</li><li>Ethylene Vinyl Acetate Rubber (High VA Density)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ethylene Vinyl Acetate (EVA) resins are categorized based on vinyl acetate (VA) content. **Vinyl Acetate-modified Polyethylene (Low VA Density)** contains less than 18% VA, offering rigidity and chemical resistance, ideal for films and coatings. **Thermoplastic Ethylene Vinyl Acetate (Medium VA Density)** has 18-40% VA, balancing flexibility and strength, used in adhesives and footwear. **Ethylene Vinyl Acetate Rubber (High VA Density)** contains over 40% VA, providing high elasticity and softness, suitable for rubber-like applications and impact modification. Each type serves distinct industrial needs based on VA concentration.</p></p>
