<p><p>The pharmaceutical colors market is categorized into synthetic, natural, and natural-identical types. Natural colors are derived from plants, minerals, or animals, preferred for safety and clean-label trends. Natural-identical colors are lab-created to mimic natural sources, combining consistency with regulatory acceptance. The choice depends on application, regulatory standards, and consumer preferences.</p></p>
