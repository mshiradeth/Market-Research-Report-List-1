<p><p>The tie-down straps market primarily features three types: Cam Straps, Over-Center Lever Cam Style Straps, and Ratchet Straps. </p><p>Cam Straps utilize a simple cam mechanism for tensioning, making them user-friendly and versatile. Over-Center Lever Cam Style Straps offer enhanced security through a lever action that locks the strap in place, ideal for heavier loads. Ratchet Straps provide superior tensioning by using a ratcheting mechanism, ensuring maximum stability for transporting heavy items, making them popular across various industries.</p></p>
