<p><p>PTFE venting in the packaging market involves two primary types: adhesive vents and vents without backing material. Adhesive vents are designed to adhere directly to surfaces, providing a reliable seal while allowing gases to escape, ensuring product integrity. In contrast, vents without backing material are typically lightweight and versatile, easily integrated into various packaging designs. Both types are essential for maintaining optimal pressure and preventing spoilage in packaging applications, especially for food and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
