<p><strong>The High Resolution Inkjet Printers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Continuous Inkjet Printer</li><li>Drop-on-demand Inkjet Printers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The high-resolution inkjet printer market is primarily divided into two types: continuous inkjet printers and drop-on-demand inkjet printers. Continuous inkjet printers operate by continuously flowing ink through a nozzle, allowing for high-speed printing suitable for industrial applications. In contrast, drop-on-demand inkjet printers release ink droplets only when needed, providing greater control and often resulting in finer detail, making them ideal for high-quality graphics and images. Both types cater to different segments based on speed, quality, and application requirements.</p></p>
