<p><p>The commercial waterproofing membranes market includes various types, notably modified bitumen membranes and synthetic polymer membranes. Modified bitumen membranes are asphalt-based, enhanced with additives to improve flexibility, UV resistance, and durability, making them suitable for flat roofs and low-slope applications. Synthetic polymer membranes, such as TPO and PVC, offer superior waterproofing, UV resistance, and energy efficiency. These membranes are lightweight, easy to install, and provide long-lasting protection, catering to diverse commercial building needs for effective moisture management and structural integrity.</p></p>
