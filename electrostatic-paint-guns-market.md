<p><ul><li>Pneumatic Electrostatic Paint Guns</li><li>Airless Electrostatic Paint Guns</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electrostatic paint guns market is primarily segmented into two types: pneumatic and airless electrostatic paint guns. Pneumatic electrostatic paint guns utilize compressed air to atomize paint particles, ensuring even coverage and reduced overspray, making them ideal for intricate designs and surfaces. In contrast, airless electrostatic paint guns use high pressure to spray paint without air assistance, providing a thicker coat and faster application on larger areas. Both types enhance efficiency and finish quality in various painting applications.</p></p>
