<p><p>The film coating system market comprises two main types: aqueous film coating and non-aqueous film coating. Aqueous film coating uses water-based solutions, making it environmentally friendly and suitable for heat-sensitive substances. It's commonly employed in pharmaceuticals and food applications to enhance appearance and stability. Non-aqueous film coating, on the other hand, utilizes organic solvents for faster drying and improved coating performance, making it ideal for industrial applications where rapid processing is critical. Both types enhance product quality and functionality.</p></p>
