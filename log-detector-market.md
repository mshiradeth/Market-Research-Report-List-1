<p><p>The Log Detector market comprises several types, including Logarithmic Type and RMS (Root Mean Square) Type detectors. Logarithmic Type detectors are designed to handle wide dynamic ranges, providing a logarithmic output that compresses high signal levels for easier processing. RMS Type detectors measure the effective value of varying signals, delivering consistent output for both AC and varying DC signals. Other types may include envelope detectors or peak detectors, each tailored for specific applications in signal processing and measurement.</p></p>
