<p><p>The Floral Flavours Market can be categorized into three types: natural, organic, and artificial. Natural floral flavors are derived from real flowers through processes like distillation or extraction, preserving their authentic essence. Organic floral flavors, on the other hand, come from flowers grown without synthetic pesticides or fertilizers, ensuring environmentally friendly production. Artificial floral flavors are chemically synthesized to mimic the taste and aroma of natural flowers, often offering consistency and cost-effectiveness, but lacking the depth of natural varieties. Each type serves distinct consumer preferences and applications.</p></p>
